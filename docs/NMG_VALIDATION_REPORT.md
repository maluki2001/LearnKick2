## Flagged Questions Requiring Expert Review

### 1. Rheinfall Question (AMBIGUOUS)

**ID:** `3f3fb20a-20b2-4932-96da-c0665221c42e`

**Question:** "Der Rheinfall ist der grösste Wasserfall in Europa."

**Type:** True/False

**Correct Answer:** Wahr (True)

**Grade Level:** 2

**Difficulty:** 2

**Quality Score:** 70/100

**Issue:**
The statement "Der Rheinfall ist der grösste Wasserfall in Europa" is **ambiguous** because:

- ✅ **BY VOLUME:** The Rheinfall is the largest waterfall in Europe by water volume (approximately 600 cubic meters per second average flow)
- ❌ **BY HEIGHT:** The Rheinfall is NOT the tallest waterfall in Europe (only 23 meters high). Vinnufossen in Norway is 860m high.

**Recommendation:**
Reword the question to be more specific:
- Option 1: "Der Rheinfall ist nach Wasservolumen der grösste Wasserfall in Europa."
- Option 2: "Der Rheinfall bei Schaffhausen hat das meiste Wasser von allen Wasserfällen in Europa."

**Action Required:** Expert review and question rewording
