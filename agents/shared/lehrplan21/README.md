# Lehrplan 21 - Swiss Curriculum Standards

## Overview
Lehrplan 21 is the curriculum for German-speaking Switzerland, covering grades 1-6 (cycles 1-2).

## Structure
- **Cycle 1**: Grades 1-2 (Kindergarten + Primary 1-2)
- **Cycle 2**: Grades 3-6 (Primary 3-6)

## Subject Areas
- **MA**: Mathematik (Mathematics)
- **D**: Deutsch (German Language)
- **NMG**: Natur, Mensch, Gesellschaft (Nature, People, Society)
- **BG**: Bildnerisches Gestalten (Visual Arts)
- **TG**: Textiles und Technisches Gestalten (Crafts & Design)
- **MU**: Musik (Music)
- **BS**: Bewegung und Sport (Physical Education)
- **MI**: Medien und Informatik (Media & ICT)

## Competency Codes
Format: `SUBJECT.CYCLE.AREA.NUMBER`
Example: `MA.1.A.1` = Math, Cycle 1, Area A, Competency 1

## Files
- `cycle-1/` - Grades 1-2 competencies
- `cycle-2/` - Grades 3-6 competencies
